About Me

Hi, I'm Nev Gonzalez.

I'm a front-end web developer building modern websites for local businesses. My path here didn't start with code. It started with people, operations, and a decade of learning how businesses actually work.

The Beginning
After high school I attended community college, where I helped establish and lead a student business club as Vice President — organizing events, inviting guest speakers, and building real opportunities for students interested in entrepreneurship.
University of Baltimore
I transferred to UB and earned a degree in Forensic Accounting. While studying, I spent nearly a decade helping operate a family-owned restaurant in downtown Frederick, gaining hands-on experience in business operations, marketing, customer service, and day-to-day management.
The Shift
My interest shifted toward technology and digital business. I landed an internship at Salesforce, learned a lot, and realized I wanted to build something of my own. I started teaching myself web development and building websites for friends, family, and local businesses. What started as a personal challenge became a direction.
Today
I combine my background in business, problem-solving, and development to build modern websites that help businesses show up online with intention and connect with their customers.
